# IMO 2015 A1

Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of positive real numbers such that, for all $k \geq 1$,
$$ a_{k + 1} \geq \frac{k a_k}{a_k^2 + k - 1}. $$
Prove that $a_1 + a_2 + \ldots + a_n \geq n$ for every $n \geq 2$.
