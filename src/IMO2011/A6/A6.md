# IMO 2011 A6 (P3)

Let $f : \R \to \R$ be a function such that, for all $x, y \in \R$,
$$ (x + y) \leq y f(x) + f(f(x)). $$
Prove that $f(x) = 0$ for all $x \leq 0$.
